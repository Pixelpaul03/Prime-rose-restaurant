About Us

About Company

Prime Rose Goa, nestled in the scenic locale of Sangolda, Goa, stands as a testament to culinary excellence, a fact underscored by its recent Times Icon Award win. Since its inception in 2013 by Mr. Saleem Khan, Prime Rose Goa has been enchanting diners with its diverse and delectable steak offerings, set against a backdrop of traditional Portuguese ambiance.

Mr. Saleem Khan, commenting on the Times Icon Award, states, “This award is a recognition of our journey at Prime Rose Goa, where we strive to create a dining experience that is memorable not only for our steaks but also for our diverse culinary offerings, including our freshly made pastas.”

Our Mission

Our vision is to offer the best steaks in Goa and expand our business in Goa to the beach side in the future.
